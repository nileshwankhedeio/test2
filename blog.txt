                  <h2 class="blog-title" id="section1">
                    How AI and Automation Are Transforming Agriculture in 2025
                  </h2>
                  <span class="line-break"></span>
                  <span class="line-break"></span>
                  <p>
                    Agriculture has entered a new era in 2025, driven by
                    Artificial Intelligence (AI) and smart automation. At
                    Nitroxen Tech, we are building solutions that combine IoT
                    sensors, AI models, and farm automation systems to help
                    farmers optimize yields, reduce waste, and make agriculture
                    more sustainable.
                  </p>

                  <span class="line-break"></span>
                  <ul class="key-points">
                    <li>
                      <strong>Smart Irrigation & Moisture Detection</strong>
                      <p>
                        Water scarcity is one of the biggest challenges in
                        modern farming. With soil and moisture detection
                        systems, farmers can now monitor soil health in real
                        time. Our AI-powered irrigation solutions ensure crops
                        get the exact amount of water they need—no more, no
                        less.
                      </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                      <strong>Fertilizer Automation</strong>
                      <p>
                        Overuse of fertilizers affects both crop quality and the
                        environment. Nitroxen Tech’s fertilizer automation
                        systems distribute nutrients efficiently based on soil
                        data, reducing costs and boosting productivity.
                      </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                      <strong>Open & Closed Farm Automation</strong>
                      <p>
                        From greenhouses to open fields, automation helps
                        farmers remotely control temperature, humidity, and
                        equipment. This creates consistency in crop growth and
                        reduces manual labor.
                      </p>
                    </li>
                    <li>
                      <strong>AI in Crop Prediction</strong>
                      <p>
                        By analyzing weather patterns, soil quality, and past
                        yield data, AI predicts the best crop cycles and harvest
                        times. This not only increases output but also minimizes
                        losses.
                      </p>
                    </li>
                  </ul>

                    Ahead: By integrating AI, IoT, and automation, we aim to
                    make farming more productive, eco-friendly, and accessible
                    to every farmer in India and beyond.
